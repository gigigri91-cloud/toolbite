QR Code Generator

Generate a QR code instantly from any URL, text, phone number or email. Download as PNG with one click.

Privacy: QR codes are rendered entirely in your browser — your text is never sent to ToolBite servers.

How to use

  1. Type or paste your URL, text, phone number, or email in the box above.
  2. Choose a size and error correction level, then click Generate.
  3. Download the PNG or right-click the image to copy it directly.

Use cases

  • Business cards & print

    Add a QR code to flyers or cards that links to your website or contact info.

  • Wi-Fi sharing

    Encode your Wi-Fi credentials so guests scan to connect without typing.

  • Event & product links

    Create scannable codes for event tickets, menus, or product pages.
