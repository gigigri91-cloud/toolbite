QR Code Generator

Generate a QR code instantly from any URL, text, phone number or email. Download as PNG with one click.

Privacy: QR codes are rendered entirely in your browser — your text is never sent to ToolBite servers.

How to use

  1. Type or paste your URL, text, phone number, or email in the box above.
  2. Choose a size and error correction level, then click Generate.
  3. Download the PNG or right-click the image to copy it directly.

Use cases

  • Business cards & print

    Add a QR code to flyers or cards that links to your website or contact info.

  • Wi-Fi sharing

    Encode your Wi-Fi credentials so guests scan to connect without typing.

  • Event & product links

    Create scannable codes for event tickets, menus, or product pages.

Frequently asked questions

What can I encode in a QR code?
URLs, plain text, email addresses, phone numbers, and Wi-Fi credentials. Any text up to a few thousand characters can be stored, though shorter content produces simpler, easier-to-scan codes.
Is this QR code generator free?
Yes — completely free, no account needed, no watermarks, no download limits. Generate and download as many PNG QR codes as you need.
What does the error correction level mean?
It determines how much of the QR code can be obscured while still scanning correctly. Low (L) gives a smaller, simpler code. High (H) adds redundancy — useful for print where a logo might overlap the code.
