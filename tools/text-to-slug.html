Text to slug generator online

Create clean, hyphenated URL slugs for blog posts, products, and landing pages directly in your browser.

Privacy: Slug generation runs locally in your browser. Your titles and draft copy are not uploaded to ToolBite.

Slug output

How to use

  1. Paste a headline or phrase.
  2. Review the generated slug (lowercase, hyphenated).
  3. Copy into your CMS, router, or static site config.

Use cases

  • Blog permalinks

    Keep URLs short and readable.

  • Product pages

    Align slug with primary keywords.

  • Multilingual titles

    Strip accents and unsafe characters.
