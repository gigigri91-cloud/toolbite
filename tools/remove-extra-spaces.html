Remove Extra Spaces

Fix double spaces, messy indents, and blank lines from PDFs or the web.

Collapse spaces turns any run of spaces, tabs, or line breaks into a single space. Use Trim each line to strip leading/trailing spaces per line. Remove empty lines deletes lines that are only whitespace.

Why clean whitespace?

Copied content often includes invisible double spaces, odd line breaks, or empty paragraphs. This tool helps you normalize text before publishing, coding, or sending emails—without sending your content to a server.
