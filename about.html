About ToolBite

Building fast, free, and secure tools for the modern web.

Our Mission

We believe that essential utility tools shouldn't cost money, shouldn't be cluttered with confusing interfaces, and most importantly, shouldn't compromise your privacy.

🔒 Privacy First

Our tools process data directly in your browser using JavaScript. We don't save your text, code, or calculations to any external servers.

⚡ Blazing Fast

Hosted on global CDNs, ToolBite is designed to load instantly. No waiting, no buffering. Just get your work done.
