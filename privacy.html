Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

At ToolBite.org, accessible from https://toolbite.org, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document describes the types of information that is collected and recorded by ToolBite and how we use it.

2. Local Processing (No Data Storage)

Most of our tools process data entirely within your browser. When you use tools like our JSON Formatter, Word Counter, or Image Compressor, your text, code, or files are not sent to our servers. All calculations and transformations happen locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

3. Log Files

ToolBite follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is used for analyzing trends, administering the site, and gathering demographic information.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, ToolBite uses cookies. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed. We use this information to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and other information.

5. Google AdSense & DART Cookie

Google is one of our third-party advertising partners. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to toolbite.org and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

6. Third-Party Privacy Policies

ToolBite's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information, including their practices and instructions on how to opt out of certain options.

7. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms.
