Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

At ToolBite.org, accessible from https://toolbite.org, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by ToolBite and how we use it.

2. Local Processing (No Data Storage)

Most of our tools process data entirely within your browser. When you use tools like our HTML Minifier, Base64 Encoder, or Word Counter, your text or code is NOT sent to our servers. All calculations and transformations happen locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

3. Log Files

ToolBite follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, ToolBite uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed. We use this information to optimize the users' experience.

5. Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to www.website.com and other sites on the internet. You may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

6. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
