ToolBite Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

At ToolBite.org, accessible from https://toolbite.org, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document describes the types of information that is collected and recorded by ToolBite and how we use it.

2. Local Processing (No Data Storage)

Most of our tools process data entirely within your browser. When you use tools like our JSON Formatter, Word Counter, or Image Compressor, your text, code, or files are not sent to our servers. All calculations and transformations happen locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

3. Hosting and Log Files

ToolBite is delivered through third-party static hosting and CDN infrastructure. As with most websites, those providers may process basic request logs such as IP address, browser type, date/time, referrer, and requested pages in order to serve, cache, secure, and troubleshoot the site.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

ToolBite itself does not use a custom analytics backend or user account system. However, third-party services embedded on the site may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies for advertising, fraud prevention, or widget behavior.

5. Google AdSense & DART Cookie

Google is one of our third-party advertising partners. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to toolbite.org and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

6. Third-Party Privacy Policies

ToolBite's Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party advertisers, widget providers, hosting providers, or external websites. We advise you to consult the respective privacy policies of those services for more detailed information, including their practices and instructions on how to opt out of certain options.

7. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms.
